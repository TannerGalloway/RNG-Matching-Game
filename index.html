Pac-Man Guessing Game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There is Pac-man and three ghost below. By clicking on pac-man or the ghost you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score gose above the random number.

The value of pac-man and the ghosts is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the values of pac-man and the ghosts.
